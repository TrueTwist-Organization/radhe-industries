Comparison

Monoset vs. Submersible

✅ Choose Monoset When:

  • Water source is above ground (tank, canal, well)
  • No borewell available or needed
  • Domestic / garden / small farm use
  • Easy surface installation required
  • Single-phase power supply only
  • Budget-friendly option needed

🔵 Choose Submersible When:

  • Water source is deep underground (borewell)
  • High head / deep lift required (50m+)
  • Large farm or industrial water supply
  • Continuous high-volume pumping needed
  • Three-phase power available
  • Silent operation preferred
